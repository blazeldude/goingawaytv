@goingaway.tv

This research project will be looking at what is beyond the prescribed boundaries of video game levels.

Commonly referred to as the out of bounds, these are spaces within digital worlds that are not normally accessible to the player. Using hacks, free cam systems and data mining I will be venturing into these areas myself to reveal what we can learn about video game construction and why these impossible spaces are so fascinating. By using computational tools such as self running bots and data scrapers I will begin to create a quasi archive of these out of bounds areas and examine how they change our perceptions of digital environments. I will then create artworks that engage with these ideas and present the archived spaces side by side with the research. While I am not entirely sure yet what form these pieces will take, I will explore interactive documentary, gamifying the archive, interventions (placing new objects/modifying existing ones) and virtual performance from within the out of bounds.
