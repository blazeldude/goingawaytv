This time it's personal

You may remember Going Away.tv, you may not. It came out of us acknowledging that within our contemporary media landscape – there is too much choice. Goingaway.tv was an antidote to this, where you could log on and tune out – we chose the content for you to enjoy. Now we’re asking you to do the same for us. Previously a 24/7 stream of Artists’ moving image, our renewed concept will take us fully into the live space. We will watch all of your submissions, no exceptions. The watching process will itself be streamed live, however long it takes. For our sanity, all works submitted must be 10 mins or under. Moving image, short films, video art - we want to see it all.

Deadline for submissions: 11/08/2025

Love,
The Director Generals

Submission guidelines:

Please send a link to your video using Youtube, Vimeo or a public Google Drive file.

By submitting your video you consent to have it watched during the Going Away.tv: Locked in live stream and featured on our website (fully credited ofc) for the duration of the project.

All submitted videos will be featured.

Restrictions:

  • No Copyright sound
  • No nudity
  • No graphic violence
  • 10 mins or under
