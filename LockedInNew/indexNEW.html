Going Away.tv: Locked In [LIVE 17/01/2026]

Don't change the channel, adjust your set, or scroll past. Fuck it... we'll do it live.

The Director Generals called upon artists far and wide to send in their moving-image works, and they did. All 117 of them.

Now they're going to watch every single one, LIVE, however long it takes.

You are invited to watch along as this highly scientific experiment explores the irreversible effects of being locked in a room with an obscene amount of video art.

This marathon will be hosted by Artist Marc Blazel and Writer/Curator Alexander Harding. Expect a blend of critique, chat, live segments, sleep deprivation and whatever else happens when you're truly locked in.

Going Away.tv: Locked In will be live all day (and probably night) on the 17th January 2026, right here at Going Away.tv.

About Going Away.tv

Going Away.tv is an online platform that previously hosted live streamed artists' moving image as a 24/7 stream. Conceived as an embassy for the 2019 edition of the Wrong Biennale, It was later reconfigured as a series of live events at Arebyte Gallery, London. This is its evolution.

Director generals: Marc Blazel and Alexander Harding.
